ul
<ul> An unordered list is used when the items inside the list do not have a specific order or the order is not important.
ol
<ol> An ordered list is used when the items inside the list have a specific order or the order is important.
em
<em> a string of emphasized, slightly more important text.
q
<q> a small quotation embedded within other content.
cite
<cite> a citation for another source, often used with quotations.
