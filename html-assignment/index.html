About Me

Greetings, everyone! My name is Yue Liu. I am a UI/UX Designer based in Ottawa, ON. I use design to explore, understand, and engage with the world. I'm passionate about creating seamless and innovative experiences, driven by a deep passion to enhance daily life for individuals.

Beyond the realm of design, I find joy in venturing out into the world to capture its beauty and weirdness. In my creative pursuits, you might catch me crafting posters or illustrating pasta using markers. On quieter days, you'll find me doing yoga in the park
